One markup, many devices, less headache!

Features:

  • Inspired by 960grid and 960grid fluid
  • Sass/Scss Grid System
  • Design just one html markup
  • Choose if you want fixed or fluid template, it mantains the same proportions on large screens
  • If fixed you can choose your favourite size (not only 960px)
  • If fixed you can choose the margins between columns
  • Adapt content on tablets (ex iPad ecc), landscape or portrait
  • Adapat contento on smartphones (ex iPhone, Android ecc)

This library is just to study css/scss/html possibilities

What should it do?

Stretch your window to get iPad portrait size or iphone or reload this page with your tablet or smartphone.
The gris system will be adapted to your screen. This is not so new but here you can do it with one single scss file and choose if your wrapper should be fixed or fluid... check it out on GitHub
